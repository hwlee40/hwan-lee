I’m a student at the Rhode Island School of Design currently pursuing a BFA in graphic design.

I believe good design can act as a bridge between disparate communities, cultures, and generations.

Having received a largely American education but a South Korean by nationality, I feel that I’ve been paving my own path as a Asian-American but also an Immigrant designer. I want to take advantage of my unique position between two cultures and create designs that feel natural to anyone regardless of their background .

Recently I’ve been developing new skills and challenging myself to pick up various tools that could potentially be useful for future projects and clients. This website was designed & coded by me in an attempt to familiarize myself with front-end web development. Built with HTML, (S)CSS, and JavaScript. Bundled and optimized for the web with Parcel bundler .
